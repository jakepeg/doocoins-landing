What are the best chores for a 6 year old?

A 6 year old is developmentally ready for making their bed, feeding a pet, and clearing their plate. Predictable routines and small rewards help children this age follow through with less parent prompting.

FAQ

What chores can a 6 year old do?

Most can handle simple room and table tasks with supervision.

How many chores should a 6 year old have?

Two to four short chores per day is a practical range.

Should chores be daily at age 6?

Yes, daily repetition usually builds habits faster.

What rewards work best?

Small immediate rewards plus a weekly milestone bonus.
