We aspire to empower children with financial wisdom, cultivate savings habits, make informed investment choices, shoulder responsibility, and to equip parents with the tools needed to foster sound financial behaviours in their children.

DooCoins is in its early stages, an MVP taking its first steps. Our primary focus has been on constructing a robust blockchain-based product that encompasses fundamental elements such as task and reward lists, a secure wallet, and goal-setting capabilities.

In our development pipeline, we have exciting features in store, including a dedicated kids' version of the app. This version allows children to submit completed tasks and request rewards, which can then be verified by parents using the parent app.
