# Chores By Age (5-14) - Combined Reference

## Age 5

### Summary
A 5 year old is developmentally ready for putting toys away in the toy box or storage boxes, placing dirty clothes in the washing basket, and setting napkins on the table. These short tasks build consistency, attention, and family contribution skills when repeated daily with simple rewards.

### Parenting Tip
Use picture checklists and keep requests one step at a time. This reduces overload and increases completion consistency.

### Chores

- Put toys away into the toy box or storage boxes (DooCoins reward: 3 coins)
- Put dirty clothes in the washing basket (DooCoins reward: 3 coins)
- Set napkins at dinner spots (DooCoins reward: 4 coins)

### FAQ

- **Q:** Should 5 year olds have chores?
  **A:** Yes. Simple chores support responsibility and confidence at this age.
- **Q:** How long should chores take at age 5?
  **A:** Usually 2 to 8 minutes per task works best.
- **Q:** Should rewards be money at age 5?
  **A:** Points or coins are often easier for young children to understand than cash.
- **Q:** What if my child refuses chores?
  **A:** Start with one very small task and reward completion immediately.

## Age 6

A 6 year old is developmentally ready for making their bed, feeding a pet, and clearing their plate. Predictable routines and small rewards help children this age follow through with less parent prompting.

### Parenting Tip
Tie chores to anchors like breakfast and dinner so routines feel automatic instead of negotiable.

### Chores

- Make bed each morning (DooCoins reward: 4 coins)
- Feed pet with supervision (DooCoins reward: 5 coins)
- Clear own plate after meals (DooCoins reward: 4 coins)

### FAQ

- **Q:** What chores can a 6 year old do?
  **A:** Most can handle simple room and table tasks with supervision.
- **Q:** How many chores should a 6 year old have?
  **A:** Two to four short chores per day is a practical range.
- **Q:** Should chores be daily at age 6?
  **A:** Yes, daily repetition usually builds habits faster.
- **Q:** What rewards work best?
  **A:** Small immediate rewards plus a weekly milestone bonus.

- Fold and stack towels (DooCoins reward: 6 coins)
- Wipe kitchen counters after meals (DooCoins reward: 6 coins)
