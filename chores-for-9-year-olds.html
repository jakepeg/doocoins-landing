What are the best chores for a 9 year old?

A 9 year old is developmentally ready for hoovering one room, sorting laundry by colour, and packing school lunch items. Children at this age benefit from clearer ownership and measurable expectations.

FAQ

What chores can a 9 year old do?

Most can do room care, laundry prep, and lunch setup tasks.

How often should chores be checked?

Once daily plus a weekly routine review is usually enough.

Should I tie chores to allowance?

Many families keep base chores separate from extra earning chores.

How do I avoid power struggles?

Set the rules in advance and keep them consistent.
