    Connect to the Parent App

    The current version of the parent app is targetted at mobile devices. To keep it fully decentralised we have designed it as a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means you can access it directly from your phone's web browser and add it to your homescreen for a true app experience.

    To get started, simply follow these steps:

    1. If you're viewing this page on your mobile device click here, otherwise scan the QR code below using the device camera.
    2. Follow the on screen instructions to install the app
    3. Launch the app by clicking the DooCoins icon on the home screen.

    If you encounter any issues, have questions, or would like to share your thoughts, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at hello@doo.co
