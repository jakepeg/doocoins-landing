What are the best chores for a 12 year old?

A 12 year old is developmentally ready for running a basic laundry cycle, sweeping and mopping small areas, and helping with meal prep. This is a strong age for increasing independence and weekly responsibility.

FAQ

What chores can a 12 year old do?

Most can complete moderate household tasks with limited supervision.

How much supervision is needed?

Usually moderate oversight plus occasional quality checks.

Should 12 year olds have weekly goals?

Yes, weekly goals teach planning and time management.

What rewards fit this age?

Higher points, privileges, or larger weekly rewards can work well.
