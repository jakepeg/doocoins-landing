DooCoins : Kids Rewards

With DooCoins, children are rewarded for their good behaviour, completing chores, and personal achievements. DooCoins not only motivates kids but also teaches them important financial literacy and organisation skills. By setting goals and earning DooCoins, children can redeem them for fun rewards.

DooCoins stands out as a Web3 decentralised app (dApp) built on blockchain technology, ensuring privacy and security. Unlike traditional Web2 platforms where your data is the product, with DooCoins, you own both the data and the product.
