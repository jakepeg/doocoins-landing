What are the best chores for a 13 year old?

A 13 year old is developmentally ready for cleaning a bathroom, managing laundry loads, and prepping simple meals. At this age, chore systems should emphasise reliability, ownership, and quality standards.

FAQ

What chores should a 13 year old do?

Laundry, bathroom cleaning, and meal prep support are common fits.

Should teens earn more for chores?

Yes, reward scaling should reflect complexity and consistency.

How do I enforce deadlines?

Set visible due dates and consistent outcomes for missed tasks.

What if quality drops?

Require correction before payout and reinforce standards clearly.
