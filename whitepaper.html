    Whitepaper

    Introduction

    DooCoins is a rewards system designed to help kids develop essential financial literacy, organizational skills, and responsibility. Through DooCoins, children can earn rewards for positive behavior, completing chores, and achieving personal goals, all within a secure and transparent Web3 ecosystem. Powered by blockchain, DooCoins offers a fully on-chain, secure environment where kids can learn how digital currencies work and parents can feel confident in the integrity of the platform.

    Originally launched as a Web2 app with over 27,000 users, DooCoins has been reimagined as a Web3 decentralized application (dApp) and recently launched in its MVP version, gaining early traction with around 100 early adopters. Leveraging blockchain technology, DooCoins aims to be a child’s first introduction to digital currency—a technology that will likely become increasingly embedded in their future.

    This whitepaper outlines the purpose, vision, and strategy behind DooCoins, providing a deep dive into our unique solution, platform architecture, tokenomics, and future roadmap. By bridging rewards and financial education with the power of blockchain, DooCoins is poised to empower young people with the knowledge and skills necessary for a digital-first future.

    Vision

    The vision for DooCoins is to transform how children learn and practice financial literacy. In a world where digital currencies and decentralized systems are becoming commonplace, it’s critical for kids to understand the value of money, savings, and goal-setting from an early age. DooCoins equips children with hands-on experience in a safe, monitored environment, teaching them not only the importance of earning and saving but also giving them an early introduction to the potential of Web3 technology.

    Through DooCoins, children will learn by doing: setting goals, completing tasks, and managing their digital currency earnings. By creating a digital ecosystem that connects learning and real-world applications, DooCoins aspires to inspire a generation of financially savvy and responsible young people.
