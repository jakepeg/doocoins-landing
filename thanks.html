    Thanks for becoming a DooCoins Early Adopter

    Your participation in this early stage is crucial to helping refine and enhance the app to create the best possible experience for children and their parents.

    To keep the app fully decentralised we have designed it as a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means you can access it directly from your phone's web browser and add it to your homescreen for a true app experience.

    To get started, simply follow these steps:

    1. On your mobile device, in Safari (iOS) or Chrome (Android) go to app.doo.co
    2. Follow the instructions to install the app
    3. Launch the app by clicking the DooCoins icon on the home screen.

    We're committed to continuous improvement, and your feedback is essential in shaping its development. If you encounter any issues, have questions, or would like to share your thoughts, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at hello@doo.co

    Once again, thank you for your participation. We are excited to embark on this journey with you and look forward to hearing your valuable feedback.
